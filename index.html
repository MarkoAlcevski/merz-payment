About the Apartment

Your private retreat, elevated by Sky Wellness

Merdz Spa & Fitness Sky Apartments is a private, self-contained apartment in Cevahir Sky City — one of Skopje’s most sought-after addresses. To make every stay more complete, our collaboration with Sky Wellness gives guests complimentary access to its pool, sauna, steam room and fitness center, all just steps from the apartment within the same complex.

Every detail is designed around rest. Soft linens, warm lighting and calm, uncluttered rooms create a space where you can slow down — whether you’re here for a weekend escape, a longer stay, or simply a place to unwind after exploring the city.

Calm & Privacy

The entire apartment is private — no shared spaces, no shared schedules. Just a quiet place to recharge.

Sky Wellness Guest Privileges

Complimentary wellness access and 10% off massages, offered to every guest through our collaboration with Sky Wellness.

Premium Location

Set within Cevahir Sky City, close to the centre of Skopje, with secure parking on site.
